Andrew Masucci

Bio

Andrew Masucci currently works as a Data and Policy Analyst at Acumen, LLC, primarily working with the Center for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS) to support the administration of the Outpatient Prospective Payment System (OPPS) and the Inpatient Psychiatric Facility Prospective Payment System (IPF PPS). Prior to Acumen, Andrew worked at Collective Health, supporting the administration of health plans for several self-insured employers.

Education

Johns Hopkins University | Baltimore, MD

B.A. in Public Health Studies | August 2014 - May 2018

Experience

Acumen, LLC | Data and Policy Analyst | April 2020 - Present

Collective Health | Plan Operations Associate | July 2018 - March 2020
